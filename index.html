OUR STORY

We are a team of machine learning and perceptual quality video bandwidth reduction researchers. After watching our first 4K video we were hooked. But we knew that for this type of content to be feasible on existing networks, someone would have to build the enabling technologies. We wanted to apply our research in reducing video bandwidth without any change in perceptual quality to the emerging field of 4K UHD and 360 VR video.

OUR VISION

Our vision is that in 5 years time, people will be watching high definition 8K 360 VR video, on next generation mobile VR headsets and mobile devices. To enable this future, VR content itself will need to have efficient processing pipelines that reduce file sizes and lower bitrates for transmission into networks, even if networks upgrade considerably. Our goal is to build the technology to do this.
